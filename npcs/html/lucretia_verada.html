Background

Lucertia is the proud owner of The Verada Inn. Unlike most Inns, The Verada doesn't have a tavern attached, it's just an Inn. She prides herself on having clean comfortable rooms at a reasonable price and does good business here. The bulk of her income, however, comes from her harem of escorts.

The Verada has a reputation as something of a "No tell, Motel". Lucretia is a stern mistress who runs a tight ship and wont take any crap from anybody. She's been at this game for a long time and knows exactly what she's doing. While friendly and gracious with customers, she won't accept any disrespect and has "security" on hand at all times to handle any problem customers or if necessary, escorts.
