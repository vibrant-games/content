Roleplaying

Introduction

The chaos of the bustling tavern is contrasted by the composed keep behind the bar. She pushes a tankard towards you.

Appearance

A beautiful but clearly weathered half-elf with noticeable battle scars. Sleek blond mylar hair. Deep brown eyes.

Expressions

"The wild ain't so wild; depending on who you are"; "The traditions of the trees tell us where we mortals truly belong"

Mannerisms

Calm and composed in movement. Light but commanding nods and gestures. Drums fingers on the bar with calm impatience.

Motivations

Katerina has resigned herself to a common life of tradition. Desires reigning in expanding technologies &amp; magicks.

Passions

Nature. Trees. Any usable product of nature as revered.
