Introduction

From the wharfside water comes a hoarse croaking voice - "WILL PELHAM BEAUTY OR GOBERNADOR WIN THIS YEAR'S DERBY?"

Appearance

Brown sleek furred skin, snout, black eyes, gilled cheeks. Webbed fingers, flippered feet, spinal ridge to his tail.

Expressions

WILL THERE BE A WAR?; DEATH RAYS TURN WHOLE CONTINENTS INTO DUST; DO YOU WANT PERFUMED BREATH? USE "FRESH" TOOTHPASTE.

Mannerisms

Wags tail underwater when excited; barks when happy or scared; performs "the newt", his underwater dance, on request.

Motivations

Gossips incessantly, and strangers gossip to him; reads every pamphlet ever printed; parrots the town crier verbatim.
