Story
Moonglow grew up wild and eventually made her way into town. She always loved dancing and quickly found that she could make a very good living as a dancer at the gentlemen's clubs in the city. She found that she could further supplement her income by providing some extra services at the clubs. Approached by the Thieve's Guild, she received a little bit of training and began to make even more money by collecting information from her customers and passing that information to the guild. Her exotic appearance and talent, have made her one of the favorites performers in town, and being very popular has made gathering information.
