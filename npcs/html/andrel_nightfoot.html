Story
Andrel grew up in a reclusive tribe of Ghostwise and was trained early as a hunter and scout. Unlike a lot of her tribe, she was seized by the wanderlust that afflicts many Halflings, she left the deeps woods and ventured out into society to make her name. Her reputation as a hunter and guide precedes her and is widely regarded as one of the best people to help travelers find their way safely through any section of woodland. Over the years she has also developed a strong distaste for orcs and their regular attempts to ambush travelers.
