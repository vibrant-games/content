Roleplaying Tips

Introduction

A young halfling woman bars your path. "Between us, a captured puma on a rope. How are we to approach each other?"

Appearance

Beautiful, immaculate, slender for a halfling; one letter pasted to each eyelid; carries a silvery fruit in one hand.

Expressions

"We are all ku"; "Your thoughts are like my dresses, the waistlines all identical"; "All dreams were dreamt long ago."

Motivations

Recovering her lost language, which she promptly forgot upon the khagan's decision to convert the Khazar halflings.
