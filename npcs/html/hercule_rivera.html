Story
<p>Hercule is the owner/operator of Hercule's Curiosity Shop. Having tried his hand at several careers throughout his lifetime, including briefly attempting to school for sorcery, he found that he excelled at sales. Using the contacts he made through his brief schooling, he created his current personae and opened his curiosity shop.</p><p> Most of the goods he carries are minor magic trinkets of no real use to anyone, but he also carries and extensive stock of materials used for the various schools of spellcasting. From holy symbols to spellbook materials, Hercule's carries it all. Now and again, he does actually manage to procure some useful magic items for sale, but those are few and far between.</p>
