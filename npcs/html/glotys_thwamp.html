Combat Tactics

Glotys will avoid combat in most cases. Should she be pressed she will first resort to calling her swarm and backing off in hopes that will suffice to waylay opponents or drive them off.

Roleplaying Tips

Introduction

Glotys loves to spread word of the quality of her honey products. She keeps busy throughout the market place selling her edible honeys in the forms of jarred honeys, jellies, crystals, and so forth, partnering with bakers, chefs, and candymakers. Glotys is seeking rare flowers to plant within her aviary range to produce even more formidable honeys.

Appearance

Relatively lean and gaunt for a dwarf; tangled black curls and chin beard; wears firm leather beekeeping uniforms at nearly all times; bright green eyes; Always wears fresh flowers in her hair

Expressions

"Can't make honey without getting stung!"; "My money is on the little things that make the world what it is. Bugs, crawlies, moss, and pebbles"; "Keep me outta the loop. I got tons to do already!"; "Bugs are far more organized than most peoples";

Motivations

Glotys is driven to discover new and exotic flora for her bees to pollinate and thus make different honies. She is also quietly motivated to quell the conflicts between races. She's convinced herself that the answer is 'Honey'.
