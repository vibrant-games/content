Background Story

 Orica Zarkova grew up in a tribe of Ondonti Orcs. Growing up with a belief in the balance of nature, celebrating strengh, survival, family and peace. She was taught healing magic early in life and has continued to pursue that into her career. She has become adept at alchemy especially the creation of healing potions, but through her research has learned to make pretty much any kind of potion, oil, acid or poison. The ultimate goal of her research is to develop what she calls The Elixir of Life; a potion that will both grant immortality as well as restore life to the dead and the undead. So far she has been unsuccessful in her pursuit, but continues her research undetered.

Personality

 Orica Zarkova Is a tall, lithe, subtly muscled, gray-skinned orc. She clearly puts considerable effort into maintaining her appearance with long, silky broan hair, pierced, pointed ears and has had her tusks filed down as to not be so prevalent. She dresses in soft stylish leather armor covered by a long lab coat. Having been away from her tribe for a long time Orica is well aware of the way that the world views her people and because of this she has become jaded and guarded in her interations with others. She is fierce and driven to accomplish her goal. Anyone who questions her intent or ability is met with hostility and indignance. She jealously guards her research and while she enjoys the benefits that the Collegium has to offer, she fears that Oswald Vardklemp and Gerhardt Klippstein want to steal her work (they don't).
