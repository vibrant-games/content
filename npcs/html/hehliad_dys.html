Story
<p>Hehliad was born and raised in Polsgaard, the capital city of the region and where the royal magistrates reside. As a child, her parents would bring her to the numerous festivals held on behalf of the nobility and her young mind could imagine doing nothing else with her life than perform and contribute to the kind of joy experienced there. Studying her craft throughout her younger years, she caught the attention of one of the lower magistrates who enlisted her as his assistant and a discerning eye for festival talents. It wasn't long before she was given the role of Master of the Revels and took charge to create some of the most memorable events in recent history.</p>
