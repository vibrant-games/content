Roleplaying

Introduction

Yemik can be found researching a local magical anomaly, or seeks a party's help in retrieving a magical object

Appearance

Young adult half-orc with black shaggy hair. Taller than average with a thin build. Reading glasses that never seem to leave his face.

Expressions

Knowledge is power. A mistake is another opportunity to learn.

Mannerisms

Almost exclusively reading, even during conversations. Talks to himself while tracing figures in the air.

Motivations

Writing a guide to help new adventurers. Finding magical objects to help fund his expeditions.

Passions

Yemik has a keen interest in devil contracts, orc culture, and coffee

Secrets

Yemik knows how to summon a devil, though has yet to do so.

Background

Orphaned at a young age, Yemik does not remember his family. Yemik spent his days learning to read at the orphanage and eventually was requested to live at a school for the gifted. A prof. took him in as an assistant and fed Yemik's curiosities. Yemik has a desire to give the world the gift of knowledge, hoping to make a book that would be in every household.
