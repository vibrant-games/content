Roleplaying

Introduction

A shadow eyes you, twirling purple hair between fingers, a small metal file rotating between fingers in the other hand.

Appearance

Purple hair; rose-hued smock over tatami armour. Two dark flecks on each cheek. Soft, steady eyes.

Expressions

"Honk!"; "Are you my new gaggle?"; "Shift security left"; "Anyone can learn to be a goose."

Mannerisms

Cocks head to one side; fingers always dancing. When seated, taps their feet in strange, polyrhythmic cadences.

Motivations

Driven to discover the most innovative means of maintaining security for patrons. always searches for the holes in defensive strategies or even lores and stories.

Passions

Coalwater loves teaching lockpicking to ethical thieves ("geese"); study new lock and crypto designs for fun.
