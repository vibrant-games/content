Background Story

 Nicodeamus Dumas is the eldest grandson of Anastasia Dumas and the defacto leader of the Dumas Family cult. Pretty much since birth, Nicodeamus was raised by his grandmother Anastasia, and grew in her twisted image. As a child, his only friends were the lesser devils that serve his grandmothers patrons. They taught him their ways, their magic, their bargains, their deceptions, and they taught him well. He grew to become a consumate grifter, conman and liar. They taught him to play their games and to gamble. When he came of age, his grandmother introduced him to her benefactor, to whom he took an oath, brokered a deal and was rewarded with a sizeable amount of power upon completion of his given task. At a very early age he also became fascinated with a new technology slowly appearing across the realm, firearms. His doting grandmother encouraged his new hobby and acquired him the best pieces that could be found. He mastered handling them, repairing them, shooting them and eventually even building his own. He is one of the formost masters of firearms in existence, and when he took on his patron, he was gifted a pair of very special revolvers. He used his gifted weapons to carry out his side of the bargain he made, the murder of his own wayward father and retreive his mother. His father had taken his mother away from the family and turned her against their patron. Nicodeamus was charged with hunting him down, ending his life and returning his mother to his grandmother. He did so with glee and with flourish. Gunning the man down in cold blood, almost as an afterthought, after securing his mother and burning their home to the ground. Completing his assigned task, he granted significant power from his patron and has been practicing his gifts ever since. As he reached his late 20's, his grandmothers growing frailty required him to step up and take over the day to day leadership of the family. With the help of his wife/sister Irini, he has been a very successful leader, growing the family in size, power and influence.

Personality

 Nicodeamus Dumas is almost a caricature of a man. Tall and bone-thin with pale, freckled skin and wild curls of flaming red hair that he keeps tucked under a wide brimmed hat. He dresses like a dandy, with fine travelling clothes, impeccibly pressed, usually in a purple or burgundy with white gloves. He almost always has a sly, almost quizzical smile plastered across his face and entrancing violet-colored eyes. He is perpetually whistling a jaunty little tune but makes no effort to hide the sidearm on each hip under his long black coat, openly demonstrating to all that encounter him that he is always a threat. While coming off as jovial and cartoonish, Nicodeamus is sharp as a tack and thoroughly vicious. His happy demeanor is primarily for the purposes of recruitment, but also partially because he's a little on the mad side. He is endearing and charming while things are going his way, but like a true sociopath, can turn hard and cold at a moments notice. He is ruthless in persuit of his goals, but generally finds it easier to use the carrot rather than the stick to achieve his aims. He likes to use either gambling or card tricks to break the ice with potential recruits and can be very persuasive when he wants to be. With the exception of his patron and his granmother, he will tolerate absolutley no disrespect or disobedience from the other members of 'The Family' and will not hesitate to sacrifice anyone who invokes his ire.
