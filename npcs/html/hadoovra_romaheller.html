Story
Hadoovra is a mountain dwarf that has left the mountains and moved to the city. After fighting her way through architectural and engineering school, she works hard every day to prove herself in a male dominated industry. Her fierce drive to master her craft and get ahead has made her well know and respected in her vocation, but she is also well known for letting her temper get the better of her. Her strong work ethic has also lead to a bit of a drinking problem in he relaxation time, which also does not help her temper at all
