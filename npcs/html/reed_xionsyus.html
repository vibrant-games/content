Introduction

 One market booth is especially eye-catching with foreign weapons, armor, and curios . "Hail. I bring you to the East."

 Well polished red and yellow Karuta. Long grey-green hair in pony-tail. Filed down tusks. Bright blue eyes.

 To expose the West to the East. To revive the importance of tradition.

 Eastern weapons, armors, equipment, clothing, and curios.
