Introduction


A woman stands before a building, commanding two golems to hoist a sheet of forest glass snugly into the windowsill.

Appearance


Plain grey tight-fitting full-length dress. Long straight black hair tied in a tight bun. Arched eyebrows.

Expressions


"Mmmmmm"; "Mmmmm hmmmm"; "Mmmmm-mmmmm". Quut rarely says anything, preferring to roll her eyes or blink twice for "yes".

Mannerisms


Supporting one elbow with the back of one hand while smoking a long cigarillo in a silver holder in her other hand.

Motivations


Quut has worked for years to retrain and hire out golems whose masters have died, leaving behind masterless slaves.

Passions


Liberating golems from eternal servitude; her husband, Postmaster Squelch; spikes, including dangerous stiletto heels.
