Background Story

 Jedediah Dumas is the second son of Anastasia and the public face of the family. While his nephew, Nicodeamus, is the leader behind the scenes and Anastasia's favourite, Jedediah is the public face of the the family. He handles all political dealings and negotiations between the family and any outside institution, be they governments, businesses or other organizations. Jedediah sits on the city council as well as the boards and committees of several other institutions including the City Watch. He will always negotiate to the benefit of the family and attempt to influence any organization he is apart of to do his family's work for them. Jedediah has also been charged as the primary caregiver for his beloved sister Delores and dotes on her as often as his other responsibilties allow. He is not happy with the horrors that have been wrought on his dear sister, but he understands that they needed to happen for the good of the family. He is, however, very unhappy with how Nicodeamus abuses and tortures his mother. He, in fact, does not like much of what Nicodeamus does, who he is or the decisions he has made for the family and believes that Anastasia should have put him in charge instead of his demented nephew. While he would never make an outright play for leadership, especially while Anastasia is still alive, he has been building his own power base in case the day should ever come. Above all else, he will do anything in his power to protect Delores.
