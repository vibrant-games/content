Background Story

 Nothuriel was a great leader of the King's Army. Rising up through the ranks along side of her beloved husband Gerrard Romkey. Their bond of love provided each other with strength and resolve. The two had waded into many a battle and conquored many a foe in the name their god, king and country, becoming regaled heroes of the realm. All until the last battle. Nothuriel had secretly heard dark voices in her head since childhood. Those voices had lead her to find and take a cursed blade called the Blood-Sword. In the pitch of battle, the Blood-Sword caused Nothuriel to lose control. Mid-battle she turned and impaled her love, the Blood-Sword absorbing his blood and soul preventing any magical healing from reviving him and restoring his life. Fiding that the army's healers, doctors and clerics could not restore Gerrard, she immediately walked away and went on a quest to find someone or something that could. Since then she has wandered the earth, searching ancient texts and ruins and temples to find a way to bring him back. She has also sworn to find and reek vengeance against the cause of the voices in her head

Personality

 Nothuriel is a broken and battered woman. She is depressed, exhausted and well on her way to madness. She no longer speaks, other than in mumbled and muffled tones and only to herself, her sword and the voices in her head. She will absolutely not be curved or distracted from her mission. She will do her best to walk away from confrontation, but if pressed will bring to bear all of her considerable martial prowess.
