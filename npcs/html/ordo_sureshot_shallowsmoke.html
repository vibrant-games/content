Roleplaying Tips

Introduction

Ordo frequents the surrounding little villages selling his vegetables and meeting with old friends.

Appearance

Ordo is 3ft 1 and very muscular for a halfling, he has shoulder length dark hair, goatee beard, and bright blue eyes

Expressions

"Lets get in there then folks" "Come on twinkle toes"

Mannerisms

Facepalming when people say or do stupid things. Ordo chews tobacco when thinking up a plan of action or idea.

Motivations

Wants to make the world a better place, all for the greater good

Passions

Family and friends

Secrets

Was trained as a scout by elves and is highly regarded as a master scout and an amazing shot with either bow or knives.

Background

From the Harfoot subrace of Hobbits. Ordo was brought up by his parents but his parents died during a goblin raid, he was found by elves who took him in for several years and taught him their skills, he was esp good at scouting and was naturally as good as the best Elves. He showed an effortless ability with bow and knives.
