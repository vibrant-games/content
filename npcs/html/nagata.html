Roleplaying Tips

Introduction

A slender spire of an orc spills gears and belts from a jute sack at your feet. "Ah'm sorry, y'all, ah'm fallin' apart!"

Black skin with tints of purple and ochre, smouldering eyes, thin lips, well-dressed with leather high-stacked boots.

Expressions

"Howdy"; "Y'all"; "Ah reckon"; "Ain't no thang"; "Pitchin' woo"; "By golly"; "Here's a florin for your troubles."

Motivations

He is motivated to cultivate his intelligence and talents with magic in spire of his heritage.

Passions

Brilliant with devices (clocks, thieves' tools, autocarpentry). Quiet, unassuming, straightforward and honest.
