Background Story

 Ulther Hapwath was originally a human but has spent most of their adult life experimaneting on and augmenting his own body in search of some distorted view of human perfection. A skilled spell slinger and hunter, they hunt and capture many specimens of various creatures to study and experiment on. They are a brilliant biologist with an exceptional knowledge of many different creatures anatomy and the steady hands of a surgeon. Recruited by Gerhardt Klippstein to join the Collegium for their vast biological knowledge, their employers are slightly less than keen on the reckless abandon with which they are willing to experiment on themselves. Since they joined the Collegium, they have been contacted by a mysterious benefactor from beyond the veil. Ulther has no idea who this benefactor is, but they have been helping to guild their self-evolution and in the creation of a substance called Cytoplast, a sort of ooze that helps with the bonding of upgrades and grants temporary healing when applied to a living creature. Ulther's benefactor is in fact, the disembodied spirit Momir Vig, disgraced and deceased former Guildmaster of the Simic Combine on the Plane of Ravnica. Somehow Momir has crossed into this world and is slowly using Ulther to create Cytoplast and use it to build him a new body.

Personality

 Ulther Hapwath is tall and muscular and severe-looking with pale blue skin and long. hip length, silver/white hair. They wears dark glasses as their replaced eyes are somewhat sensative to bright lights, and a long, black leather trenchcoat that cover most of their bodily features. On closer inspection, its clear that Ulther has made significant modification to their body. Barbed skin on their hands and feet like a spider for climbing, the wings of a manta for gliding, sections of crab-like shell armoring portions of their body and a segmented scorpions tail with stinger are all faintly noticeable if someone spends enough time looking at them, although they are often using some kind of glamour (disguise self, polymorph, etc.) when in public so as not to stand out as much. Ulther is fanatical about his work and about chasing his personal concept of bodily perfection and finds it hard to comprehend why everyone isn't seeking to augment thier bodies. They are reckless and never think twice about experimenting on themselves and have little regard for the wellbeing of the other creatures that they experiment on. Thanks to the influence of their secret benefactor, they have gradually started to explore the commonly unknown arcane study of biomancy. While mostly keeping this information to themselves, they have shared some of their studies with their close friend and fellow body-modifying scientist and Collegium member Thadeus Periwinkle.
