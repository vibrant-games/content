Story
Sami is a male halfling who has been living as a female since she and her "half-sister" moved to this new city. Sami's sister is really her wife who has helped transform Sami into the women she now is. Sami works as a cleaning maid at the local inn and enjoys playing games at the inn when she's not working. Her favorite tavern game is Tak.
