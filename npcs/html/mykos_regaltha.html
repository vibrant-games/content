Background Story

 Mykos grew up in the Underdark with his tribe of Tieflings. His father, Rophelos, was the Khan and his mother, Mytha, a Queen of their kind. As war among the denizens of the Underdark continued to rage, his tribe ceased to worship thier traditional gods in favor of one of the old forgotten gods; Kezef, The Hound of Rot. As his tribe delved deeper into the following of Kezef, their raids became ruthless. Killing and wanton destruction for the sake of killing and wanton destruction. The also bagan taking prisoners to use as slaves. This didn't sit well with Mykos as he had always had a pronounced sense of fairness and justice. When he brought his concerns to his parents he was forced to fight his older brother Rophelor to the death in single combat. Mykos defeated his brother, but refused to deliver the killing blow. Mykos then escaped from his tribe, finding his way out of the Undrdark, taking himself into exile. Shortly after leaving the Underdark, he found a lone Half-Orc named Ordic and an exiled Drow, Etzio, and the three outcasts set out on the road of adventure, gaining fame and fortune over the years, becoming known as "The Forgotten". Using their new found wealth to buy a tavern, settling in the small shanty town of Phandalin, and opening Ardenthal's Rest. Recently his Brother Rophelor came back into his life, now also exiled from their tribe and escorted by, much to Mykos' surprise, a daughter that Mykos had never known existed.

Personality

 Mykos is average height but built like a brick wall of muscle. He has deep red skin, lang black hair and short-cropped beard. He has white eyes and thick curled horns like a ram. He wears short cut pants (pants of holding) with his long muscular tail hanging out the back above his hoofed feat. He also wears the trophies of his triumphs, A necklace of troll teeth, the thick, red, woolen cloak of a fallen Red Wizard and the blood-soak hat of a slain redcap over top of the specially-made helmet that fits around his horns. He also has a large tattoo of a naked lady on his left arm. Mykos is gruff, intimidating and impetuous, often acting without thinking and barrelling headlong into danger, despite his acute danger sense. Once battle commences though, there are few more violent, cunning and aggressive. Despite his tendency towards violence, Mykos also has a keenly developed sense of fairness and justice. He also has a strong aversion to authority in general, but specifically to the tenets and intervenions of the many gods of the realm. He also has a special distatse for slavers, and a genuine hatred of the Red Wizards of Thay in particular. He has also been known to go out of his way to provoke any authority figure that crosses his path, especially those who see him as lesser.
